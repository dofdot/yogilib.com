Yogilib exists to contain historical documents, treaties, records, manuscripts, letters and everything else pertaining to Nepal. I dedicate this site to the memory of Yogi Narharinath for Nepali history and culture is incomplete without his work.

This site is run by an individual and not an institution, sponsored by the individual and other individuals on a strict contribute whenever you want, how much you want basis.

This site would not be possible without Govinda Kafle. It is his mission that I am continuing here. His bhakti towards the Yogi that has now shown the world, the extent of a ahistriocity that can permeate the jana. This site exists because of Govinda Kafle.

On information management.

I had a few handwritten documents by the Yogi which included the list of all of his works and a sandhi-patra-sangraha. I have decided to transcribe the works and dedicate this place on the internet for those and other documents. This resource needs to congregate and establish itself as a store of knowledge .

To send items of concern like pdfs, typewritten text or writings contact yogilib108@gmail.com
